# Template: 15-Second Outside-to-Inside Walk-In UGC
# Use for: videos that start outside and walk into shop

## Structure:
# 0-2s: Outside at night — full body visible, says hook line, walks in
# 2-5s: Inside — smelling gesture OR first reaction to shop
# 5-9s: Picks up bag/product — shows logo to camera
# 9-12s: Picks up ice cream — tastes directly (no spoon if hand occupied)
# 12-13s: Places item down — looks at camera
# 13-15s: Chef's kiss — abrupt cutoff

## Notes:
# - Walk-in must be ONE CONTINUOUS SHOT — no cut between outside and inside
# - Phone stays in LEFT hand in selfie mode throughout — NEVER changes
# - Logo on bag/cup must face camera — never reversed
# - Frame: full body at 0-2s, waist-up from 2s onward
