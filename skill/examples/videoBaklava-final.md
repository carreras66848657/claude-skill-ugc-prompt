never reversed. This applies to every single frame.

IMPORTANT — REFERENCES: All visual references provided 
must be treated as exact fixed references. The ice cream cup 
@icecreem must appear exactly as in the reference — same cup

The shop interior @image1-inside must appear exactly as in

The shop exterior @image2-outside must appear exactly as in 
the reference — same illuminated sign, same entrance, 
same white floor tiles, same warm amber interior glow.
No colors, shapes, logos, or structural elements 
from any reference may be altered, replaced, or reinterpreted.

Vertical 9:16 authentic UGC selfie-style social media video,
iPhone front camera selfie mode held in LEFT hand throughout
the entire video — she NEVER puts the phone down,
she NEVER changes the camera angle or mode.
The camera stays in selfie mode from first frame to last frame.
Real handheld subtle micro-shake throughout.
Natural skin tones, no filters, no color grading.
Ends abruptly mid-motion like a real Instagram Reel —
no fade, no transition, just cuts out instantly.

CRITICAL — LOGO AND TEXT: All text and logos visible in 
the video — on the ice cream cup AND on the bag — must 
NEVER be mirrored or reversed at any point.
All Arabic and English text must be fully readable 
and correctly oriented in every frame.

CRITICAL — FOOD CONTINUITY: Once she eats from the ice cream,
the surface of the ice cream shows that mark for the rest
of the video. Nothing resets. What is eaten stays eaten.

CRITICAL — PHONE: The phone stays in her LEFT hand in 
selfie mode for the ENTIRE video. She never puts it down,
never flips it, never changes angle.

Setting transition — the video moves through TWO locations
in one continuous flowing shot:

LOCATION 1 (0–2s): Outside Baklava Boutique at night 
@image2-outside — illuminated white sign with gold and
burgundy branding glowing above the entrance, Arabic and 
English text on the sign fully readable, warm amber interior
light visible through the open glass door, clean white

LOCATION 2 (2–15s): Inside Baklava Boutique @image1-inside —
warm golden ceiling spot lights, marble walls with gold veins,
stone brick display counter with silver baklava trays visible 
in the background.

Character: @avatar — early 20s, warm olive skin,

Outfit: deep burgundy long-sleeve structured midi dress —
modest, elegant, sleeves to the wrist;
small gold hoop earrings,
thin delicate gold chain necklace,
a delicate gold bangle on her left wrist.

Action and dialogue sequence — ONE CONTINUOUS FLOWING SHOT:

0–2s: She is outside the shop at night @image2-outside.
iPhone front camera selfie in her LEFT hand,
held at arm's length — the frame shows her FULL BODY 
from head to feet, the illuminated Baklava Boutique sign 
and shop entrance fully visible behind her.
Her complete outfit is visible in this opening frame.
She looks directly at camera with natural warm excitement.
She speaks:
"Baklava Boutique — girmeden geçemezdim."
After speaking, she walks forward through the open glass door —
smooth and continuous — NO CUT, NO JUMP.
Audio: soft night street ambience transitioning to warm shop interior.

2–5s: She is now inside the shop @image1-inside.
Frame settles from waist upward — outfit visible.
She takes one or two slow natural steps forward.
She stops. Her RIGHT hand rises near her face —
natural smelling gesture, fingers slightly open.
Eyes close for exactly one beat as she inhales.
She says softly:
"Mis gibi kokuyor..."
Audio: warm indoor shop ambience, her soft Turkish voice.

5–9s: Her RIGHT hand picks up the branded transparent bag 
@bag_pkg from the counter.
She slowly raises it toward the camera, rotating it so 
the Baklava Boutique logo, Arabic text, and phone number 
0782238202 face DIRECTLY toward the camera lens —

"Sipariş hazır — bakın!"

Audio: her warm clear Turkish voice, shop ambience.

9–12s: Her RIGHT hand picks up the ice cream cup @icecreem.
She raises it to chest height, branded label facing the lens —
fully readable, NOT mirrored.
She holds it for one natural beat.
She slowly leans her head slightly toward the cup —
takes a small slow natural bite from the top of the soft-serve
directly with her mouth. No spoon.
Eyes close for half a beat.
"Dondurma ve baklava — sadece bir dinar."
Audio: her soft warm Turkish voice, near-silent soft-serve sound.

12–13s: She lowers the cup gently back onto the counter.
She looks at the camera — warm eyes, quiet genuine smile.
One natural beat of silence.

13–15s: Her RIGHT hand slowly brings thumb, index finger, 
and middle finger together as one pinched unit.
She raises these three pinched fingers slowly toward her lips.
She presses them gently against her lips —
holds this contact for one full deliberate beat.
Then she opens her hand slowly and gracefully,
extending her fingers outward toward the camera lens.
The clip ends abruptly on this gesture —
no fade, no transition, instant Reel cutoff.
